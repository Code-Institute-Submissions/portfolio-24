Content Writing:

While search engines use a variety of factors to rank a website or webpage, the content (including articles & blog posts) on your website is crawled by search engines to 'rank' that content. As this method is fully automatic and performed by AI, it is important to have content that is both meaningful yet keyword rich.

Keywords are terms that Google and other search engines use to 'rank' a website by evaluating it's contents and how useful your webpage may be to a user. The better and more appealing your content is, the higher rank your content will achieve. This means that your content will likely appear higher in search results, which may translate into more website traffic.

In basic terms, the better your content is the better you will rank.

This however is easier said than done. As the 'person' reading your content is essentially a computer, it is important to keep keyword densities, paragraph lenghts and other features within a standard which is awarded by search engines.

The challenge of SEO writing is to balance the content to help search engines rank it, while also remaining interesting and appealing to the reader.

If you would like to know more on how I might be able to help you with your SEO content writing, please contact me using the form at the bottom of the page.
